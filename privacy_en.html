Waska Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 Oct 2025

1) Purpose and Scope

This document explains what personal data Waska processes, for which purposes, the legal bases, retention periods, and your rights. Waska aims for data minimization and transparency.

2) Data We Process & How We Collect It

Waska does not collect personal data. No registration or account is required. We do not request or process identifying information (name, age, email, etc.).

2.1 Technical/Operational (Non‑Personal) Data

  • Device model, iOS version, locale
  • App version, performance and crash logs
  • Analysis request metadata (non‑content summaries such as date/coin type)

3) Purposes of Processing

  • Provide and improve core functionality
  • Debugging and ensuring application security
  • Manage and verify subscriptions (Apple StoreKit 2)
  • Provide customer support

4) Legal Bases

  • Performance of a contract (Terms of Use)
  • Legitimate interests (security, performance, debugging)
  • Legal obligations (Apple billing/subscription records)

5) Third Parties & Transfers

  • Apple StoreKit – Purchases and subscription verification (processed by Apple)
  • Crypto/News APIs – Market data and news feed (no personal data is sent)
  • AI Service – Minimal content strictly required to generate analyses (no personal data)

Since we do not collect personal data, no identifying information is transferred to these parties.

6) Accounts

No account is required. The App supports guest usage and does not ask for identifying information.

7) Cookies & Tracking

Waska does not track users for advertising. No third‑party ad SDKs are used.

8) Retention

  • Subscription records: as required by law and for dispute handling
  • Analysis metadata: for a reasonable period to provide the service
  • Crash/performance logs: up to 90 days

9) Security

  • TLS (HTTPS) in transit
  • Access control and authorization
  • Regular security updates

10) Your Rights

  • Access, rectification, erasure
  • Objection and restriction
  • Data portability

To exercise rights: waskadestek@gmail.com. We may ask for proof of identity.

11) Children’s Privacy

Not directed to children under 18.

12) Data Export & Deletion

  • Email request

As the app does not collect personal data, deletion requests generally relate to clearing device‑local data (e.g., caches).

13) Compliance & Transparency (5.1.1)

  • No personal data collection; no ad tracking SDKs.
  • No account requirement; guest usage by default.
  • StoreKit payments are processed by Apple; we do not store payment data.
  • Contact for data requests: waskadestek@gmail.com

14) Changes

We may update this policy; the effective date will be shown above.

15) Contact

Email: waskadestek@gmail.com

Waska processes only the minimum data required to operate the service and is committed to user privacy.
